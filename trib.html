Virat Kohli - The Run Machine

Personal Life:

Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India.

Born and raised in New Delhi, Kohli trained at the West Delhi Cricket Academy.

He made his T20I debut in 2010 and later made his Test debut in 2011.

Cricket Achievements:

- Holds the record for the fastest century by an Indian in ODIs

- Named the ICC ODI Player of the Year in 2012, 2017, and 2018

- Led the Indian cricket team to its first-ever Test series victory in Australia in 2018-19

- Became the fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in ODIs

- Known for his aggressive style of play and consistency in all formats of the game

Honours- Awards:

2013 – Arjuna Award, second highest sporting honour.

2017 – Padma Shri , India's fourth highest civilian award.

2018 – Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award, India's highest sporting honour

Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy (ICC Men's Cricketer of the Decade): 2011–2020

Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy (ICC Cricketer of the Year): 2017,2018
